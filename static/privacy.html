Privacy Policy

How AI Verify handles your data — transparent and compliant.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Data Controller

AI Verify (operated by Konstantin Gritsch)
Lübeck, Germany
Email: dpo@ai-verify.ai

We are the data controller for the personal data processed through our service at ai-act-verify.onrender.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide through our compliance check form:

  • Company name — provided by you
  • Website URL — the website you ask us to analyze
  • Company size, sector, revenue, HQ location — optional business context
  • AI system details — use cases, deployment type, risk self-assessment
  • Compliance status — existing documentation, DPO appointment

We do not collect: names, email addresses, IP addresses (beyond standard server logs), cookies, or any browsing behavior data.

3. Legal Basis (Art. 6 GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — by submitting the form, you consent to processing for your compliance report
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — improving our service and training our AI models (aggregated, anonymized data only)

4. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating your EU AI Act compliance report
  • Improving the accuracy of our compliance analysis
  • Service analytics (anonymized aggregates only)

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for marketing.

5. Data Retention

We retain compliance reports and submitted data for 30 days. After this period, all data is permanently deleted. Server logs are retained for 7 days for security purposes.

6. Your Rights (Art. 15–22 GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion
  • Restriction — limit processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dpo@ai-verify.ai. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

7. Third-Party Processors

We use the following sub-processors:

  • Render (Render Services Inc.) — hosting provider, US-based (EU Standard Contractual Clauses in place)
  • Ollama Cloud — AI model inference for report generation
  • GitHub (GitHub Inc.) — code repository and deployment

All processors are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) compliant with Art. 28 GDPR.

8. Cookies

AI Verify uses no cookies. We do not track users, serve ads, or use analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • No storage of credentials, passwords, or authentication data
  • Regular data cleanup (30-day retention)
  • Minimal data collection principle

10. EU AI Act Compliance

As an AI-powered service, AI Verify itself complies with the EU AI Act:

  • Transparency: this policy explains how our AI works
  • Human oversight: reports are AI-generated but reviewed by our team
  • Accuracy: our model is regularly validated
  • Data governance: training data is documented and bias-monitored

11. Changes to This Policy

We will update this privacy policy as our service evolves. Material changes will be announced on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
